Research your ballot!

We've partnered with BallotReady to provide a tool that gives you everything you need to know about the races on your ballot—from your state representatives to your local school board, and everything in between. Make informed choices by comparing candidates based on their stances on issues, resumé, and endorsements.
(Information on funding sources coming soon!)

Beyond research, you can also register to vote, request a mail-in ballot, making a voting plan, and see important dates and deadlines.

And remember, the online tool may vary slightly from the final ballot, and filling out your ballot here does not cast your vote!
